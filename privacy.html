Privacy Policy

Privacy is my top priority

Effective Date: March 4, 2025

Welcome to 890.blog. Your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy outlines how I handle your data when you visit my blog. Rest assured, I am committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring a secure, tracker-free browsing experience.

No Trackers

I do not use any third-party trackers, analytics services, or scripts that monitor your activity on my blog. Your browsing experience is entirely private.

No Cookies

My blog does not use cookies. You can browse freely without worrying about cookie-based tracking or storage on your device.

No Logs

I do not collect or store any logs of your visits, including IP addresses, browser details, or timestamps. Your interaction with my blog is anonymous.

No Personal Data Collection

I do not request, collect, or store any personal information, such as names, email addresses, or contact details. If you choose to contact me via email or other means, any information you provide will not be shared or stored beyond what is necessary to respond to you.

Fully Auditable

This blog is proudly FOSS (Free and Open Source Software). The source code for the site is publicly available, making it fully auditable. You can review the code yourself to verify that no trackers, cookies, or logging mechanisms are in place.

Visit the source code repository here: GitHub Repository.

Third-Party Content and External Requests

This blog strictly serves all its assets, including fonts and images, from its own server without making any automatic external requests. External links on the site require an explicit user click to initiate any web request. No part of this site will automatically send you to an external server. When you click on these links, you will be directed to third-party websites which may have their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

Since I do not collect or store any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. Your privacy is fully preserved by design.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised periodically to reflect changes in practices or operational needs. Whenever updates are made, a full-screen popup notification will appear on the site for at least 90 days to ensure you are fully informed.

In the event that I am ever compelled to collect information against my will, the site will be shut down. For confirmation that I still maintain control over the site, please visit libertycanary.ml.codes.
